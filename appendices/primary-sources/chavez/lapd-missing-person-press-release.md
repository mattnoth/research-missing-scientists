## Content

The Los Alamos Police Department issued a press release confirming that Anthony Chavez, 78, was reported missing on May 8, 2025. Deputy Chief James Rodriguez stated that officers are "actively following every lead, reviewing evidence, and coordinating with multiple agencies and local businesses" to locate Mr. Chavez.

Search efforts included thorough searches of known residences, hiking local trails, distributing flyers to businesses, reviewing surveillance footage from multiple sources, and following up on every tip received. Rodriguez described community information as "invaluable."

Chavez is described as a 5'6" tall white male weighing approximately 135 pounds who wears glasses. He was last seen May 4, 2025, and left behind his wallet, keys, cigarettes, phone, and other personal items at his residence.

Contact: Los Alamos Emergency Communications Center at (505) 662-8222.
