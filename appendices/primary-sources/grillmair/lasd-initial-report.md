## Excerpt (paraphrased)

Per the LASD Homicide Bureau information relayed through multiple media outlets:

- Deputies from Palmdale Station responded at approximately 6:10 a.m. on February 16, 2026, to the 30700 block of 165th Street East in Llano following a 911 call reporting an assault with a deadly weapon.
- Deputies found a 67-year-old male (later identified as Carl Johann Grillmair) lying on the front porch of the residence, suffering from a gunshot wound to the torso.
- LA County Fire Department paramedics attempted lifesaving measures. Grillmair was pronounced dead at the scene.
- The LA County Medical Examiner's Office identified the victim on February 17.
- LASD Homicide Bureau detectives assumed the investigation.
- Freddy Snyder, 29, of Llano, was subsequently arrested and linked to the homicide. He had been arrested earlier that day for carjacking a family member's vehicle.
- On February 18, the LA County DA charged Snyder with murder (with firearm-discharge allegation), carjacking (with firearm allegation), and first-degree residential burglary (December 28, 2025).
- Bail set at $3.175 million.
- The LASD Homicide Bureau contact number for tips: 323-890-5500. Crime Stoppers: 800-222-8477.
