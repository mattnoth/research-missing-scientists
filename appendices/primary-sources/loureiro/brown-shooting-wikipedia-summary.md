## Key Facts Extracted

### The Brown Shooting (December 13, 2025)
- Time: approximately 4:00 PM EST
- Location: Room 166, Barus and Holley Building, Brown University, Providence, RI
- Context: economics review session
- Weapon: 9mm handgun; at least 44 shots fired
- Killed: Ella Cook (Brown student, VP of College Republicans); Mukhammad Aziz Umurzokov (Uzbek American student)
- Wounded: 9 additional students
- Suspect fled before police arrived (4:05 PM on video; police responded 4:06 PM)

### The Suspect
- Full name: Claudio Manuel Neves Valente
- Born: January 22, 1977, Torres Novas, Portugal
- Education: Graduated first in class, Instituto Superior Tecnico, Lisbon (2000); enrolled Brown PhD physics (Sept 2000); leave April 2001; withdrew July 2003
- Immigration: Won Diversity Immigrant Visa lottery; U.S. permanent residency April 2017
- Last address: Ives Estates, Florida
- Employment: IT specialist for SAPO (Portuguese company)

### Connection to Loureiro
- Both attended IST Lisbon 1995-2000
- Valente graduated first; Loureiro's grades described as "average" by classmates
- Loureiro went on to lead MIT PSFC; Valente's career stalled

### Investigation
- December 17: DNA and fingerprints recovered from shell casings
- Reddit tip about gray Nissan Sentra with Florida plates proved crucial
- December 18: Arrest warrant issued; Valente found dead in Salem, NH storage unit
- Two guns found; ballistics match to both shootings
- January 6, 2026: DOJ released video confession transcripts
