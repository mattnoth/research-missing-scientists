## Content

Jason Thomas, 45, was last seen on December 12, 2025, around midnight, walking away from his home in the Murray Street and Chestnut Street area of Wakefield, Massachusetts. His wife reported him missing to Wakefield Police in the early hours of December 13.

Physical description: white male, approximately 5 feet 6 inches tall, approximately 180 pounds. Last seen wearing a white and grey or black puffer-style jacket, black pants, black sneakers with white laces, a black winter hat, and gloves.

Thomas left home on foot without his phone or wallet. He was a 46-year-old pharmaceutical researcher at Novartis, holding advanced degrees in physics, biology, and biophysics, working in drug discovery and chemical biology. He had been struggling with grief following the deaths of both parents the previous month.

Wakefield Police Chief Steven Skory deployed four on-duty patrol officers to canvass the area. By 11:00 AM, five or six canines and four drones were searching. NEMLEC resources were also deployed. No trace was found during the initial search.
