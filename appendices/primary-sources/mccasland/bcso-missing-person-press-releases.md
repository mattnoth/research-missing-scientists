## Content

BCSO issued a Silver Alert for William "Neil" McCasland, 68, a retired U.S. Air Force Major General, who was last seen at his home on Quail Run Court in Albuquerque on the morning of February 27, 2026. A repairman last interacted with McCasland around 10:00 AM; his wife returned shortly after noon to find him gone.

McCasland left behind his phone, prescription glasses, and wearable devices. Items believed to be missing from the home include hiking boots, his wallet, and a .38 caliber revolver with a leather holster.

BCSO stated: "It is unlike Neil to be out of touch from his family and friends for this length of time; but we have not given up hope."

Search efforts included canvassing more than 700 homes, drone operations, helicopter support, ground searches with Search and Rescue teams, and K9 searches. A gray U.S. Air Force sweatshirt was found approximately 1.25 miles east of his home on March 7.

BCSO is working with the FBI, Kirtland Air Force Base, and other partner agencies. Investigators are concerned for McCasland's safety due to medical reasons.
