# Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office — Statement Addressing Speculation

- **Source URL:** https://www.newsweek.com/neil-mccasland-update-sheriff-addresses-speculation-missing-general-11827036
- **Access date:** 2026-04-20
- **Publisher/Author:** Newsweek (quoting BCSO official statements)
- **Document type:** Law enforcement official statement
- **Date of document:** April 2026

## Content

The Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office issued statements addressing public speculation about William McCasland's disappearance:

On classified work connection: "At this time, we have not developed evidence establishing that Mr. McCasland's disappearance is connected to his classified work."

On the investigation posture: "We are aware of the speculation surrounding this case. As with any high-profile investigation, that kind of attention can create additional tips and information that investigators must review, but our team remains focused on the facts and on pursuing credible leads."

On ruling out scenarios: "This remains an active investigation, and we are not going to speculate or rule in or rule out possibilities without evidence."

On connections to other missing persons: BCSO told Newsweek it had no verified information establishing any connection between McCasland's disappearance and any other missing person investigation.

Investigators reported finding no evidence of foul play.
