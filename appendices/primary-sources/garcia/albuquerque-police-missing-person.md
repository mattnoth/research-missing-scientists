## Content

Steven Garcia, 48, was last seen leaving his Albuquerque home on foot on August 28, 2025, at approximately 9:00 AM. Security cameras captured him wearing shorts and a green camouflage shirt. He was carrying a handgun; police warned at the time that he may have been a danger to himself.

Garcia left behind his phone, keys, wallet, and car. He worked as a property custodian at the Kansas City National Security Campus (KCNSC) facility in Albuquerque, which manufactures more than 80% of all non-nuclear components for the U.S. nuclear weapons stockpile. He reportedly held top security clearance with broad site access.

Days after Garcia's disappearance, KCNSC reportedly launched an internal search, going through his work computers, emails, and files for clues, but nothing was found. No direct Albuquerque PD press release was located; case details are sourced from media reporting on the police report.
