## Content

The New Mexico State Police classified Melissa Shirley Casias, 53, as a "missing endangered" person. Casias, an employee of Los Alamos National Laboratory, was last seen on June 26, 2025, walking eastbound on NM-518 near Talpa in Taos County, approximately three miles from her home in Ranchos de Taos. The sighting was corroborated by security video from Kit Carson Electric Company at approximately 2:18 PM.

Earlier that day, Casias had driven her husband Mark to LANL, returned home after forgetting her security badge, and around 12:50 PM dropped off a Subway sandwich at her daughter Sierra's workplace. She left without her phone, wallet, or keys.

NMSP spokesperson Wilson Silver confirmed in August 2025: "We have no updates." Neither foul play nor voluntary departure has been ruled out. A $2,500 reward was offered.
