# Dossier: Deaths and Disappearances of U.S. Defense and Advanced-Research Scientists

## Abstract

Between June 2022 and February 2026, eleven U.S. scientists, officials, and researchers connected to defense, aerospace, nuclear, or advanced-research programs died or disappeared under circumstances that drew public attention and, eventually, federal review. This dossier documents each case using tiered sourcing and confidence ratings, evaluates nine pre-registered hypotheses, and maps verifiable connections across three analytical layers (tight, medium, and speculative).

The research finds that the cluster is substantially a media-constructed artifact. Two cases (Grillmair, Loureiro) have named suspects with strong non-conspiracy explanations supported by forensic evidence and, in one case, a video confession. One case (Thomas) involves a pharmaceutical researcher with no defense connection whose disappearance followed severe personal grief. One case (Eskridge) predates the cluster by three years and was ruled suicide, with her father publicly dismissing conspiracy theories. Among the remaining cases, McCasland and Reza present the strongest profiles for a targeted pattern given their career significance, while Chavez, Casias, and Garcia remain unexplained but lack evidence of coordination or third-party involvement.

No common operational signature, no documented professional connection between any two subjects, and no physical evidence of third-party involvement have been identified in any case. The null hypothesis (coincidence compounded by selection bias) receives moderate-to-strong support from the evidence. The foreign-intelligence hypothesis lacks strategic rationale according to nuclear security experts and is unsupported by available evidence, though it cannot be categorically excluded for the most sensitive cases. UAP-disclosure adjacency is documented only for McCasland and is narrower than public perception suggests.

An [FBI investigation](https://www.newsweek.com/fbi-investigating-missing-dead-scientists-what-we-know-11852176), confirmed by Director Patel, and a [House Oversight Committee inquiry](https://oversight.house.gov/release/comer-burlison-seek-information-on-missing-nuclear-and-rocket-scientists/) are ongoing as of April 2026. This dossier is a living artifact that will be updated as new information emerges.

## Executive Summary

### The cluster

Starting in mid-April 2026, the Trump White House (via [Press Secretary Karoline Leavitt](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/white-house-reviewing-cases-missing-dead-scientists-possible-links-11th-person-identified)) and the [FBI confirmed a "holistic review"](https://www.newsweek.com/fbi-investigating-missing-dead-scientists-what-we-know-11852176) of approximately 11 U.S. scientists and officials who have died or disappeared under unusual circumstances since 2022. The [House Oversight Committee](https://oversight.house.gov/release/comer-burlison-seek-information-on-missing-nuclear-and-rocket-scientists/) (Chairman Comer, Rep. Burlison) opened its own investigation. This dossier documents all 11 cases using the repository's tiered source taxonomy and confidence-rating system, evaluates nine pre-registered hypotheses, and maps connections across three analytical layers.

### What the research found

**Cases with resolved explanations (4 of 11):**
[Carl Grillmair](cases/grillmair.md) was shot by Freddy Snyder, a local Llano, CA resident with an escalating criminal history, charged with murder, carjacking, and burglary. [Nuno Loureiro](cases/loureiro.md) was killed by Claudio Valente, a former college classmate from Portugal harboring a documented 20-year grudge; ballistic evidence, DNA, and a video confession link Valente to both the Loureiro murder and the Brown University mass shooting. [Jason Thomas](cases/thomas.md), a pharmaceutical researcher with no defense connection, disappeared after both parents died within 90 minutes of each other; his body was found in a nearby lake with no foul play suspected by the DA. [Amy Eskridge](cases/eskridge.md)'s 2022 death was ruled a self-inflicted gunshot; her father, a retired NASA engineer who was her closest collaborator, publicly stated there was "nothing suspicious."

**Cases that remain unexplained (5 of 11):**
[Anthony Chavez](cases/chavez.md) (retired LANL employee, specific role unknown) left his Los Alamos home on foot without wallet, keys, or phone in May 2025 and has not been found. [Melissa Casias](cases/casias.md) (LANL administrative assistant) was last seen walking on a Taos County road in June 2025; both phones were reportedly factory-reset (a family-sourced claim not confirmed by law enforcement). [Monica Jacinto Reza](cases/reza.md) (NASA JPL Director of Materials Processing, co-inventor of the Mondaloy nickel superalloy for rocket engines) vanished while hiking in the Angeles National Forest in June 2025; a Find a Grave memorial was anomalously created four days later listing her as deceased. [Steven Garcia](cases/garcia.md) went missing from Albuquerque in August 2025; his claimed KCNSC employment traces to a single anonymous source and remains unverified. [William Neil McCasland](cases/mccasland.md) (retired USAF Major General, former AFRL commander, former SAPOC executive secretary) disappeared from his Albuquerque home in February 2026, leaving behind his phone and glasses but taking his wallet and revolver; his wife reported he had experienced mental fog, anxiety, and memory loss.

**Cases with undisclosed circumstances (2 of 11):**
[Michael David Hicks](cases/hicks.md) (JPL research scientist) died in July 2023 and [Frank Maiwald](cases/maiwald.md) (JPL principal researcher) died in July 2024. Neither has a publicly disclosed cause of death. Reports that no autopsy was performed lack traceable sourcing to the LA County Medical Examiner. Both worked primarily in unclassified domains.

### Key analytical findings

**No inter-case connections documented.** Despite institutional co-employment at JPL ([Reza](cases/reza.md), [Hicks](cases/hicks.md), [Maiwald](cases/maiwald.md)) and LANL ([Chavez](cases/chavez.md), [Casias](cases/casias.md)), no source has documented that any two subjects knew each other, worked together, or shared a project. This is the single most significant finding against a targeted-pattern hypothesis.

**Geographic and temporal clustering are real but largely explained by institutional geography.** Four of 11 cases are in New Mexico (home to LANL, Sandia, Kirtland AFB) and four in LA County (home to JPL, Caltech). Eight of 11 occurred within a 10-month window (May 2025 to February 2026), though earlier cases were retroactively added to the cluster.

**The foreign-intelligence hypothesis lacks strategic rationale.** Nuclear security experts ([CSIS](analysis/foreign-intel-layer.md), [NTI](analysis/foreign-intel-layer.md)) argue persuasively that the U.S. defense-science workforce is too large and redundant for individual targeting to achieve strategic effect. The historical precedent most often cited (Israeli targeting of Iranian nuclear scientists) operated under conditions that do not apply to the U.S. context. No intelligence agency has indicated foreign involvement.

**UAP-disclosure adjacency is narrower than public perception.** [McCasland](cases/mccasland.md) has a documented but contested connection to Tom DeLonge's advisory circle; his wife characterizes it as brief, unpaid consulting for fiction writing. [Eskridge](cases/eskridge.md)'s exotic-physics claims are unverified. Extending UAP connections to other subjects requires guilt-by-institutional-association reasoning.

**Expert opinion is divided but the skeptical camp has the stronger factual basis.** Former FBI officials and Congressional investigators lean toward treating the pattern as potentially significant. Nuclear security analysts at nonpartisan think tanks, who have direct expertise in how defense programs work, lean toward coincidence. The sitting [DOE Secretary stated](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/deaths-disappearances-scientists-staff-government-labs/) the department has "not found anything alarming yet."

### What remains unknown

The [FBI investigation](https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/white-house/fbi-will-look-connections-deaths-disappearances-scientists-rcna332397) is ongoing. If classified intelligence indicators exist (intercepted communications, HUMINT reporting, forensic evidence), they would not be publicly disclosed. The absence of public evidence does not prove absence of classified evidence. The [House Oversight Committee](https://oversight.house.gov/release/comer-burlison-seek-information-on-missing-nuclear-and-rocket-scientists/) has requested briefings by April 27, 2026. Key gaps include: [Chavez](cases/chavez.md)'s specific LANL role; whether [Casias](cases/casias.md)'s phones were truly factory-reset (not confirmed by NMSP); the explanation for [Reza](cases/reza.md)'s anomalous Find a Grave memorial; [Garcia](cases/garcia.md)'s actual employer; [McCasland](cases/mccasland.md)'s medical status; and causes of death for [Hicks](cases/hicks.md) and [Maiwald](cases/maiwald.md).

## Connection Analysis Summary

The cross-case analysis identifies connections at three layers:

**Tight layer (documented, verifiable):** Two institutional clusters (LANL: [Chavez](cases/chavez.md), [Casias](cases/casias.md); JPL: [Reza](cases/reza.md), [Hicks](cases/hicks.md), [Maiwald](cases/maiwald.md)) and three geographic clusters (NM, LA County, Massachusetts). No documented professional connection between any two subjects (no shared project, lab, or confirmed acquaintance). Temporal clustering is real: 8 of 11 events in a 10-month window.

**Medium layer (research-domain adjacencies):** [Reza](cases/reza.md) and [McCasland](cases/mccasland.md) have the strongest domain overlap (aerospace propulsion/materials), though no documented personal connection. Behavioral parallels (leaving without belongings) appear in 5-6 cases but are common in personal-crisis scenarios. Phone factory-reset ([Casias](cases/casias.md)) and suppressed cell data ([Reza](cases/reza.md)) are the most operationally anomalous details.

**Corkboard layer (speculative):** UAP-disclosure connections are documented only for [McCasland](cases/mccasland.md) (DeLonge email, wife's partial confirmation). Foreign-intelligence and "silencing" narratives lack evidentiary support.
